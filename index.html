Scale Worlds

SCALE-VR is an NSF funded project which stands for Scale Cognition through Advanced Learning Environments in Virtual Reality. We are creating and evaluating an immersive virtual environment (Scale Worlds) which enables the understanding of size, number, and numeracy through embodied cognition. Scale Worlds is a mélange of design features, education theory, and human factors.

Motion tracking interfacing VR

We build interfaces for virtual reality that use human body as inputs. The interfaces are practical tools for controller-free interactions in virtual reality and support agency of avatars for recognizing implicit biases.

Holographic

We use mixed reality to empower humans through embodied training and learning. Embodied experiences are created to support worker posture changes and learner knowledge gains. The study also leads to establish framework for evaluating 3D user interfaces.
